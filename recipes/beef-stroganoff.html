Beef Stroganoff


What is it?

This delicious russian dish consists of beef and egg noodles in a creamy sauce. Said to be created by the Stroganoff family in the 1800s. It is also sometimes served with rice instead of noodles, and/or with mushrooms as well!

Ingredients you'll need

  • 1 (8 oz) pack egg noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 (10.5 oz) can fat-free condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Saute ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned and crumbly; 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, 7 to 9 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  3. Drain and discard any fat from the cooked beef. Stir condensed soup and garlic powder into the beef. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Remove beef from the heat. Add egg noodles and stir to combine. Stir in sour cream and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
